ABOUT

About

Tobias Lorsbach is a Mainz, Germany-based artist known as Logic Moon. Releases appear on labels such as Whitelabrecs, Archives, Archipel, DRONARIVM, Ambientologist, Shimmering Moods, and his own Logic Moon Music imprint, across physical and digital formats. After years in minimal techno, a field-recording excursion sparked the ambient debut "Herbst11". Later works include "I See Planets" (2019) and "Music For Film Vol.1" (2024). His mastering work supports labels and artists worldwide, and collaborations include "Inseln" (2022) and "Ewiger Wald" (2024) with Henrik Meierkord, plus projects with Sven Laux and Scarless Arms.
